      Frequently Asked Questions About PDF Compression

      How do I compress a PDF file for free?

      Simply drag and drop your PDF into the upload area above, choose a compression preset (Email Ready, Web Optimized, Print Quality, or Maximum Shrink), and click "Compress PDF." Your file is processed entirely in your browser — fast, free, and private. Download the compressed file instantly.

      Is this PDF compressor really free with no limits?

      Yes. PDF Buzz Pro's compressor is 100% free. There are no file size limits, no daily compression caps, no watermarks, and no forced signups. You can compress as many PDFs as you need, any time.

      How much can I reduce my PDF file size?

      Reduction depends on the content. Text-heavy PDFs typically achieve 60–80% size reduction. Image-heavy or scanned PDFs can be reduced by 40–70%. In optimal cases, our AI compressor achieves up to 95% size reduction.

      Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

      Absolutely. Our batch compression feature lets you upload and compress multiple PDFs in one go. Each file is processed individually with a detailed summary table showing original size, compressed size, reduction percentage, and processing time for every file.

      Will compressing my PDF reduce its quality?

      Our AI-powered compression is designed to minimize visible quality loss. You can preview the compressed result side-by-side with the original before downloading. Use the "Print Quality" preset for near-lossless compression or "Maximum Shrink" when file size is the top priority.

      Is my PDF safe? Are my files uploaded anywhere?

      Yes, completely safe. Unlike most online PDF compressors, our tool processes everything client-side in your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server. This makes PDF Buzz Pro one of the most private PDF compressors available.

      What's the difference between Smart AI and Manual modes?

      Smart AI Compression automatically analyzes your PDF's content (pages, images, text ratio, scanned vs. digital) and applies the best settings. Manual mode gives you full control over compression level, image quality, metadata removal, font subsetting, and structure optimization.

      Does it work on mobile phones and tablets?

      Yes. PDF Buzz Pro is fully responsive and works on iOS, Android, and any modern mobile browser. You can compress PDFs directly from your phone or tablet — no app installation required. The interface adapts to any screen size with full touch support and dark mode.
