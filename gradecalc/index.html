Quick Start Guide

1. Select Your Grading System

Choose between weighted grading, points-based, or letter grades in the Calculator Settings.

2. Add Your Grade Items

Fill in your assignments, exams, and other graded items with their scores and weights.

3. Calculate Your Grades

Click "Calculate Grades" to see your current and projected performance.

4. Use What-If Analysis

Project how future assignments might affect your final grade.

5. Get AI Suggestions

Review personalized study tips based on your current performance.
