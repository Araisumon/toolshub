Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert JPG to PNG online for free?

Upload your JPG image to Image Buzz, select PNG as the output format from the dropdown, and click Convert Images. Your PNG file is ready to download instantly — no registration or payment required. All conversion happens on your device.

Can I batch convert multiple images at once?

Yes! Drag and drop multiple images into the upload area, or click Browse Files to select several at once. After conversion, download each file individually or save them all as a single ZIP archive.

Is this image converter really free?

Yes, Image Buzz is completely free. No hidden fees, no registration, and no limits on how many images you convert. We support the tool through non-intrusive advertisements.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All processing uses HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript running entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device. The tool even works offline once the page is loaded.

Can I convert iPhone HEIC photos to JPG?

Yes! Upload your HEIC photos from iPhones and iPads, choose JPG as the output format, and click convert. The conversion runs locally in your browser with no quality loss.

How do I make a favicon ICO file?

Upload your logo, select ICO as the output format, choose the 32×32 preset from resize options, and convert. Image Buzz creates a properly formatted ICO favicon ready for your website.

What image formats are supported?

Output: JPG, PNG, WEBP, AVIF, GIF, BMP, ICO, SVG. Input supports these plus HEIC, HEIF, and TIFF. That's 8 output formats and 11+ input formats for maximum flexibility.

How do I resize images during conversion?

Enable the Resize checkbox, enter dimensions, and lock the aspect ratio. Choose from 12 presets: Instagram (1080×1080), YouTube (1280×720), Facebook (1200×630), favicon (32×32), and more.
