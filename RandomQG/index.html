How to Use This Quote Generator

1. Check the Quote of the Day, displayed in a language based on your location, for daily inspiration.

2. Select a language from the language dropdown to view quotes in that language.

3. Choose a category from the category dropdown to get quotes on specific topics.

4. Click "New Quote" to get a fresh, random quote instantly.

5. Copy quotes to your clipboard or share them directly on social media.

6. Use the "Speak Quote" feature to listen to the quote. For Bengali, Arabic, and Hebrew, an English voice may be used if native voices are unavailable. For best results, use Chrome or Edge and ensure language voices are installed on your device.

Each quote is carefully selected to inspire, motivate, and provoke thought.
